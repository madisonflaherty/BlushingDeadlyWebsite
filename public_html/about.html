About

Blushing Deadly Games is an independent game development team based in Eastern Pennsylvania that currently consists of two full-time members! For more information on a team member just click on their photo below!

We are currently working on our first game called Distraint which we are hoping to release commercially for the PC market through steam!

If you want to learn more about our team or just want to talk about game development in general, feel free to head over to our contact page or email us at contact@blushingdeadly.com!
